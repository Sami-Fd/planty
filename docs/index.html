How It Works

Three simple steps to diagnose your plant

1

Capture a Leaf

Take a clear photo of the affected plant leaf using your camera, or select one from your gallery.

2

AI Analysis

The on-device MobileNetV2 model processes the image and identifies disease patterns in seconds.

3

Get Results
